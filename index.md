# Presentation

Following the success of the four first editions, we propose the **5th
edition** of the ICPR workshop on **Reproducible Research in Pattern
Recognition**.

RRPR 2024 is intended as both a participative course on reproducible research
and a practical workshop.

# Call For Papers

Two kinds of contributions are expected (PDF available
[here](https://rrpr2024.sciencesconf.org/data/callForPapersRRPR24.pdf)).

## Track 1: RR Frameworks
Papers describing frameworks, libraries, reproducibility experiences...

## Track 2: RR Results
Authors describe detailed reproducibility aspects (implementations,
limitations, future work, etc.)

Topics can overlap with ICPR tracks (geometry, ML, CV, biomedical, etc.)
