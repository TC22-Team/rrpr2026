Workshop on Reproducible Research in Pattern Recognition

Presentation

Following the success of the five first éditions, we propose the 6th edition of the ICPR workshop on Reproducible Research in Pattern Recognition.

RRPR 2026 is intended as both a short participative course on the Reproducible Research (RR) aspects, leading to open discussions with the participants, and also as a practical workshop on how to actually perform RR. In addition, another key goal for gathering the research community is to further advance the scientific aspects of reproducibility in pattern recognition research.

This workshop is of interest for all ICPR participants and attendees since it allows to handle various topics not restricted to one specific fields. The reproducibility is an important topics in general and particularly good for PhD students and young researchers to learn "good habits".

The workshop should follow an on-site and will study on the possibility to accept online presentations.

Note: RRPR 2026 will publish its own separate post-proceedings (springer LNCS, confirmation actually pending), distinct from the ICPR 2026 proceedings.
